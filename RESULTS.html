New York City water is safe children

The water in New York City is safe. Schools, parents and school staff should rest assured that drinking water is of the highest quality in the world and meets or exceeds all federal and state standards. Water tests in the city exceed 600,000 times a year. The Department of Energy works with other urban agencies to ensure that our students have safe drinking water in schools.
